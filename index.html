About
Universal Community Developers is a community based non-profit organization. We are an alliance of concerned citizens geared towards giving back, paying it forward and creating change in one youth, one family and one community at a time, while fostering a universal development process and creating safe environments for all.
Community
We help simply because we care and are dedicated to establishing pathways to success for our society and we owe it to our children, families, community, and nation to step up and change where change is needed.
Society is a mirror of its citizens. Every citizen is responsible for society. So we learned that if you don't like the circumstances and conditions that you are in, then it is upon your shoulders to change that reality.
