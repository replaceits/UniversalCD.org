Universal Community Developers
Since 2013, UCD inc. has been working to provide at risk children, their families and our communities with valuable informational services in a effort to to raise awareness of the damaging impact that crime, victimization and neglect has on our children, families and communities.
Our face may not be the most familiar on the block but our Richmond VA. based non profit has been quietly positioning itself as the cornerstone of the community. With one foot firmly planted in the child advocacy side of our mission and the other dug in for a long stay in our community, UCD inc. is poised to usher in a Universal Development Process that aims to foster change in one youth, one family and one community at a time.
We know that no one organization or agency can possibly address all the issues that negatively impact our children, families and community. So we must tap a much broader and deeper well of talent and commitment to build a shared vision of success among the public, private, non-profit and community sectors. We look forward to working towards our common goal of community development and only by working together will we truly have the necessary impact to effect change.
About
UCD inc. is a community based charitable non-profit organization founded by Richmond VA. native and reformed youthful offender Coker Robinson in 2013 after taking part in a prison youth mentorship program sponsored by VVA Chapter 682. Equipped with a plan to pay it forward, he has lead UCD's efforts by working alongside concerned citizens and other reformed offenders towards a common interest: Community Development.

UCD is 100% volunteer and it's activities are primary focused on providing resources, assistance and mentorship to our disadvantaged children who may need a little extra attention and support to realize their latent potential. We combat the impediments face by our youth and their families with comprehensive initiatives placing an emphasis on education, social skills and community services.

We believe that all of us are duty bound to seeing to it that our children (rich & poor) live for an ensured future rather than in a lifetime of neglect. In the interest of Community Development, our constituency and public safety, we strive to work together with other community members to ensure supportive communities that keep children safe and help families thrive.

UCD's staff volunteers are but a microcosm of the extraordinarily selfless people who are doing their part everyday and are truly dedicated to preserving the integrity of our youth, families and communities.

Community
We help simply because we care and are dedicated to establishing pathways to success for our society and we owe it to our children, families, community, and nation to step up and change where change is needed.
